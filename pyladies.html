Pyladies Nigeria

This is a meet up group for anyone involved or interested in the python open-source language. Whether you use it professionally or haven't touched a terminal before, all levels are welcome. We plan to have informal sessions, workshops and study groups to learn python through small projects, as well as talks.

PyLadies is an international mentorship group with a focus on helping more women and genderqueers become active participants and leaders in the Python open-source community. Our mission is to promote, educate and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, events, and social gatherings. PyLadies also aims to provide a friendly support network for all women and genderqueers, and a bridge to the larger Python world.
